SUNN O)))

Sunn O))) is named after the Sunn amplifier brand, whose logo includes a circle next to the "SUNN" banner
with waves heading off to the right (the band uses this logo in the cover art of many releases).

In interviews, Stephen O'Malley stated that the name was also chosen as a play on the name of the band Earth
(pioneers of drone music throughout the 1990s), reasoning that Sunn O))) revolved around Earth.

They call their style "Life Metal".
